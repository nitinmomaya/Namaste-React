Q5. What is Virtual DOM?

-> React uses the virtual DOM as a strategy to compute minimal DOM operations when re-rendering the UI. It is not in rivalry with or faster than 
   the DOM! The virtual DOM provides a mechanism that abstracts manual DOM manipulations away from the developer, helping us write more 
   predictable code.

Q6. What is reconciliation in react?
